Tunisia's Got Talent

Project Info:

Tunisia's Got Talent is a desktop application that helps people to share their talent in various fields and to get noticed by recruiters and talent hunters, in order to get jobs.

My task in this project was to build a store where users can buy Tunisi's got talent exclusive items and clothes.
