Leaf Management Web Application

Project Info:

As part of their digitalization process, the Computer Center of the Tunisian Ministry of Finances wanted to build a full web application, using MEAN architecture, to facilitate leave requests for its employees , and leave management for its administration through a dashboard.

Project Details:

  • Company:Computer Center of the Tunisian Ministry of Finances
  • Industry:Web Development
  • Frameworks & Technologies:Angular, Nodejs, Express.js, MongoDB, HTML5, CSS3, Bootstrap, Javascript, Typescript
  • Date:June 2021 - August 2022
