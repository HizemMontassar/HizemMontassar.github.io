Project Info:

ToDaily is a web application that helps users and compagnies to manage their personnal or professional projects. This application is accompanied with :

- Google Chrome Extension: Task clipper that helps users to capture actions from any web page while surfing, and add these actions, as tasks, on their boards.

- Slack Application: It helps users to manage their tasks and boards via Slack (messaging app designed for business that connects people and professionals to the information they need)
